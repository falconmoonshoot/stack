Falcon Moonshoot

makes it easier than ever to send video messages, collect video responses, and get notifications at each step of the way.

  1. Login with your credentials
  2. Your contacts are automatically imported
  3. Select the contacts you would like to send a video message
  4. Each recipient is sent a link to a personalized landing page
  5. Recipients are able to respond with video
  6. Response video is automagically transcribed and prioritized for your viewing
